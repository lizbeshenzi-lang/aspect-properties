Property handled with the care it deserves

Aspect Properties was founded in 2012 by two agents who left larger firms for the same reason: the volume model is incompatible with genuinely good advice. When an agent has thirty listings, no single one of them gets proper attention. We limit ourselves deliberately.

We operate across W11, W1, W2, W8, and SW3. We know these streets well enough to tell you which side of the road gets afternoon sun and which buildings have sound management companies. That knowledge is what we are paid for.

Request a valuation

We offer free market appraisals for owners considering a sale or letting. The valuation takes around an hour and includes a written comparable analysis. There is no obligation to instruct us.

We also advise buyers and tenants on value — if you are considering an offer and want a second opinion, we are happy to provide one.

Discretion guaranteed
All valuation enquiries and property details are treated as strictly confidential. We do not share client information with third parties under any circumstances.
